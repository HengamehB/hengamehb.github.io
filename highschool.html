Applying to US Undergrad (For Everyone)

Applying to schools like MIT from abroad can feel like a complete mystery. Unlike most domestic systems, the US application is holistic—your grades matter, but so does your story, your curiosity, and your impact.

Here, I break down the major components of a successful international application:

  • The Essay: How to find your voice and tell your story.
  • Standardized Tests: Dealing with the SAT/ACT and English proficiency exams.
  • Extracurriculars: Quality over quantity.
  • Financial Aid: Finding schools that are "Need-Blind" for international students (like MIT, Harvard, etc.).
